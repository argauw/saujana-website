Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to produce a video from start to finish?
The timeline for video production varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple social media video can take 1-2 weeks, while a full production with scripting, filming, and post-production may take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do I need to provide all the content, or can you help with copywriting and creative concepts?
We handle everything! From concept development to content creation, our team of creative professionals will collaborate with you to ensure the final product aligns with your brand vision and goals.
What factors influence the cost of video production and pricing?
Pricing depends on several factors including the type of video, production length, complexity of editing, talent requirements, location, and special equipment needs. We offer competitive pricing and will provide a customized quote based on your specific needs and budget.
How working with Saujana Project will look like?
Working with Saujana Project follows a conversational flexible process to ensure the creative direction and production captures and delivers the best quality. We start by understanding your needs, goals, and vision. From there, we develop a creative approach, including Creative Direction & concept delivery, and handle full production with transparent communication throughout.
What other services does Saujana Project offer? Do you have a package offer?
Besides specialized visual production (video and photography), Saujana Project offers a range of creative and marketing services including Design, Branding & Creative Strategy, Digital Marketing & Social Media Management, Website Design & Development with SEO, Content Strategy & Storytelling Guidance, and Market Research & Trend Analysis. Each service is tailored to help brands enhance their presence, engage their audience, and drive results. We offer both individual services or as an integrated service package.
